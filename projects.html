My group and I had to not only make sure that the robot was able to move well, but we also had to work on the code sent to the robot. The robot used some principles of AI to have the robot follow this path.

Another project I worked on in my Artificial Intelligence class was a tictactoe game that you could play against the computer! My partner and I used the principle of heuristics and the minimax algorithm to create an AI that was capable of playing tictactoe against us. In this picture you can see some of the min nodes and their heuristic values which is how the computer chooses the "best" choice for itself. This project was really interesting as it helped me understand how AI knows what the "best" option is!
